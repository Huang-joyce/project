ABOUT US

EW Packaging is located in Los Angeles, CA. We are a co-packaging company that specializes in blister and bottle packaging for the nutraceutical, vitamin and supplement industries. We provide full turn-key packaging services from formulating and blending to packaging! Our goal is to provide you with the highest quality products. We want your ordering experience with us to be effortless and trouble-free. We understand by helping you, it helps us advance our business goals too.

FAST TURNAROUND

EW Packaging has been certified in the use of LEAN Manufacturing Practices in order to improve productivity and eliminate unnecessary waste. Our goal is to always provide you with a quick and an efficient turnaround time as much as possible.
